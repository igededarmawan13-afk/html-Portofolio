The Best Movies According to Gede Darmawan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

Spirited Away (2001): A 10-year-old girl named Chihiro accidentally enters a mysterious and magical world ruled by spirits and strange creatures. Her parents are transformed into pigs after eating enchanted food, and Chihiro must find a way to save them. She takes a job at a bathhouse run by the witch Yubaba, where she faces many challenges and meets friends like Haku, a boy with a hidden past. Through courage, kindness, and determination, Chihiro learns to navigate the spirit world and ultimately finds a way to free her parents and return to the human world, forever changed by the experience.

Ex Machina

Ex Machina (2015):A young programmer named Caleb is invited to the secluded home of tech CEO Nathan to participate in a groundbreaking experiment. His task is to evaluate Ava, a highly advanced humanoid AI, and determine if she truly possesses consciousness. As Caleb interacts with Ava, he becomes emotionally drawn to her and begins questioning Nathan’s ethics, intentions, and even his own role in the experiment. The story unfolds into a tense psychological thriller about manipulation, control, and the blurred line between human and machine-leading to a shocking and thought-provoking conclusion.

Drive

Drive (2011):A quiet, unnamed Hollywood stunt driver who moonlights as a getaway driver becomes entangled in a dangerous underworld. After befriending his neighbor Irene and her young son, he agrees to help Irene’s husband with a heist meant to clear old debts. But when the job goes wrong, the driver is pulled into a violent spiral of betrayal and gangsters. Forced to protect Irene while evading ruthless criminals, he must rely on his skills behind the wheel and his own brutal determination-leading to a haunting and tragic showdown.
